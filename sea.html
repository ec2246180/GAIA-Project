Sea Life

The oceans are teeming with diverse life, ranging from tiny organisms to the enormous blue whale. Phytoplankton play a crucial role by producing about half of the world's oxygen through photosynthesis.

Human impacts

Human activities harm the oceans in various ways. Abandoned fishing nets harm marine life, while ships cause oil spills and transport species to new places, making them invasive. Mangrove forests are cleared for development, and plastic waste is a major problem.

Climate Change

Climate change, driven by human activities releasing high levels of greenhouse gases, is significantly impacting the oceans. In 2018, the oceans experienced their warmest year on record, resulting in various consequences such as altered colors, rising sea levels, and more intense storms.
